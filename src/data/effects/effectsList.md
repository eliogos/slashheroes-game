### Player-based
- <span style="color: #ef4444;"><strong>☠️ Poisoned</strong></span>:<br>
  Loses HP each turn or action.

- <span style="color: #ef4444;"><strong>🩸 Bleeding</strong></span>:<br>
  Takes damage until healed or bandaged or bleeding has stopped.

- <span style="color: #ef4444;"><strong>🔥 Burning</strong></span>:<br>
  Fire damage over time; may spread.

- <span style="color: #ef4444;"><strong>🧊 Frozen</strong></span>:<br>
  Movement speed and attack speed are reduced.

- <span style="color: #ef4444;"><strong>⚡ Shocked</strong></span>:<br>
  Chance to skip a turn or fail an action.

- <span style="color: #ef4444;"><strong>💢 Weakened</strong></span>:<br>
  Reduced strength and physical damage.

- <span style="color: #ef4444;"><strong>😮‍💨 Exhausted</strong></span>:<br>
  Stamina drains faster and recovers slower.

- <span style="color: #ef4444;"><strong>🤢 Nauseated</strong></span>:<br>
  Causes queasiness, lowers focus, and may interrupt actions.

- <span style="color: #ef4444;"><strong>😵 Confused</strong></span>:<br>
  Accuracy drops or controls/actions become unreliable.

- <span style="color: #ef4444;"><strong>👁️ Blinded</strong></span>:<br>
  Lower hit chance and perception.

- <span style="color: #ef4444;"><strong>🤐 Silenced</strong></span>:<br>
  Cannot cast spells or use magic skills.

- <span style="color: #a855f7;"><strong>🕯️ Hexed</strong></span>:<br>
  Afflicted by a curse that negatively alters multiple stats.

- <span style="color: #22c55e;"><strong>✨ Blessed</strong></span>:<br>
  General positive modifier to multiple stats.

- <span style="color: #22c55e;"><strong>💚 Regenerating</strong></span>:<br>
  Restores HP gradually over time.

- <span style="color: #22c55e;"><strong>💨 Hastened</strong></span>:<br>
  Increases action speed, dodge, or turn frequency.

- <span style="color: #22c55e;"><strong>🛡️ Fortified</strong></span>:<br>
  Reduces incoming damage.

- <span style="color: #22c55e;"><strong>🎯 Zen</strong></span>:<br>
  Boosts accuracy, crit chance, or spell precision.

- <span style="color: #f59e0b;"><strong>😡 Berserk</strong></span>:<br>
  Increases damage but lowers defense.

- <span style="color: #22c55e;"><strong>🍀 Lucky</strong></span>:<br>
  Improves crits, loot rolls, or dodge chance.

- <span style="color: #22c55e;"><strong>🪨 Stoneskin</strong></span>:<br>
  Grants temporary armor or flat damage reduction.

- <span style="color: #ef4444;"><strong>🤏 Shrunken</strong></span>:<br>
  Smaller size, lower power, possibly harder to hit.

- <span style="color: #f59e0b;"><strong>📈 Gigantism</strong></span>:<br>
  Higher strength/health, lower mobility.
