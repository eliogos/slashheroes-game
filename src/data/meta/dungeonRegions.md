# Regions
Dungeon regions affect rooms generated per channel. They act as top-level conditions for all rooms.
<br> Effects from region conditions scale with the level of the player.

> [!NOTE]
> Regions are assigned to servers.

## Attributes
Factors that define the region.

### Temperature `enum`
Temperature affects player stamina drain, equipment durability, and modifiers fire/ice.
<br> Each race differ in heat/cold tolerance.

* ### Freezing
    > [!NOTE]
    > MIN: -20
    > MAX: -10
